Facilities

Welcome to Margarets Pub. Here's a look at our world-class facilities.

Outdoor Dining Area

Experience alfresco dining in our beautifully landscaped outdoor dining area. Perfect for enjoying a meal under the stars.

Indoor Dining Area

Our indoor dining area offers a warm and welcoming environment, perfect for casual dining or special occasions.

Bar Area/Pokies

Sip on your favorite drink in our well-stocked bar area. With a range of local and international beverages, there's something for everyone.

Fire Pit Area

Enjoy a cozy evening by our fire pit, a perfect spot for socializing and relaxing with a refreshing drink in hand.

Kids Playground

Our kids playground is equipped with safe and fun play equipment, ensuring your little ones enjoy their time at Margarets Pub.

Pool Area

Our pool area offers a refreshing oasis for you to relax and unwind. Swim a few laps or simply lounge by the pool with a drink from our bar.
