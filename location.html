Our Location

Whether you're a local resident or a traveller seeking an authentic North Korean experience, we invite you to visit Margarets Pub and become a part of our extended family. Situated at the peak of Masikryong mountain, we are only a 15-minute drive from the heart of the city 15km up Pol Pot road. Our friendly staff are ready to welcome you with open arms and make your time with us truly special. In case you have difficulty finding us or need directions, please don't hesitate to contact us at 0477248880 or email us at containmentzone.staff@gmail.com. We're always here to assist you and look forward to your visit, promising an unforgettable experience.
