Welcome to Margarets Pub!


Our Story

Margarets Pub has been a beloved gathering spot for locals and visitors alike since its establishment in the heart of the winter wonderland. Named after our beloved family matriarch, Margaret, we continue her legacy of providing warm hospitality and mouthwatering cuisine. Our cozy log cabin-style pub offers a respite from the chilly weather, where friends and families can come together, share stories, and create cherished memories.

Exquisite Cuisine

Indulge in a delectable menu that showcases both international and local flavors. From hearty pub classics to traditional Korean delicacies, our talented chefs use the finest locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that will satisfy your cravings. Whether you're in the mood for savory comfort food or want to explore the rich culinary heritage of North Korea, our menu has something for everyone.

Signature Drinks

Quench your thirst with our carefully crafted selection of beverages. We take pride in our extensive drink menu, featuring a wide array of beers, wines, spirits, and handcrafted cocktails. Sip on our signature mulled wine or warm yourself up with a steaming cup of our specialty hot chocolate infused with local flavors.

Cozy Ambiance

Step into our welcoming pub and be enveloped by the rustic charm of exposed wooden beams, crackling fireplaces, and soft lighting. Our cozy seating areas are perfect for intimate conversations or larger gatherings. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the stunning views of the snow-capped mountains while savoring your meal.

Events and Entertainment

Margarets Pub is not only a place to enjoy great food and drinks but also a hub of entertainment and live performances. From live music nights to trivia competitions, there's always something happening to add excitement to your evening. Check our event calendar regularly to see what's in store during your visit.

Visit Us

Whether you're a local resident or a traveler seeking an authentic North Korean experience, we invite you to visit Margarets Pub and become a part of our extended family. We are conveniently located in the winter mountains of Masikryong, just a short distance from Wonsan City. Our friendly staff is ready to welcome you with open arms and make your time with us truly special.
